About

Welcome to MemeRank, where dreams become memes, or something like that. Created by 4 Northeatern Second Year CS students during the Fall Hackathon Husky Hacks to commemorate their true memer friend Ethan March. Memes uploaded here are ranked based on a scale created by Ethan (see image below) and is the end all be all of meme rankings.

How it Works

We use the Clarifai API to recognize uplaoded images and based on tags that Clarifai recognizes, returns a rating. The site is hosted on a DigitalOcean droplet with a LAMP stack and Jeet as the framework for this amazing site.
